* **Definition**

    A unit simplex is the convex hull of $n$ basis points: $S_n^{(0)}=\text{conv}\\{e_1,\ldots,e_n\\}$

    A projective simplex $S_n$ is a convex hull of $n$ vectors $Z_i\in \mathbb{P}^{n-1}$: $S_n=\text{conv}\\{Z_1,\ldots,Z_n\\}$.
